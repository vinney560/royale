TL;DR: We do not sell your data. We only use cookies for authentication. Your privacy matters to us.

Data We Collect

ROYALE collects minimal data to provide and improve our services. We believe in data minimization — we only collect what is necessary.

Personal Information

  • Email address — when you sign up for notifications or create an account
  • Username — if you choose to create a profile
  • IP address — for security and to prevent abuse

Usage Data

  • Pages visited — to understand how users interact with our site
  • Features used — to improve our tools
  • Device information — browser, OS, screen size (for optimization)

We do NOT collect: Financial information, location data (unless provided by you), biometric data, or any sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us deliver a better experience. Here is what we use it for:

  • Authentication — to keep your account secure
  • Service improvement — to make our tools faster and more reliable
  • Communication — to send you important updates (only if you opt in)
  • Security — to prevent fraud and abuse
  • Analytics — to understand how our tools are used (anonymized)

We do NOT sell your data. We never share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Cookies

ROYALE uses cookies only for essential functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Essential Cookies

  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in
  • CSRF tokens — for security (protects against cross-site request forgery)
  • Preference cookies — to remember your settings (e.g., dark mode preference)

No tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts that collect personal data.

You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Here is how we protect your information:

  • Encryption — all data transmitted via HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
  • Access control — only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular audits — we review our security practices regularly
  • Data minimization — we only store what is necessary
  • Secure storage — data is stored in secure, encrypted databases

We use industry-standard security measures. Your data is safe with us.

Your Rights

You have rights over your personal data. Under GDPR and CCPA, you can:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectify — correct inaccurate data
  • Delete — request deletion of your data
  • Restrict — limit how we use your data
  • Portability — transfer your data to another service
  • Object — opt out of certain data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date below
  • Sending an email notification (if you are subscribed)
